Welcome to our experiment! In this experiment, you will see scenes of toy characters performing actions.

We are interested in how you describe them, so listeners, who don't see this change, will be able to understand what is happening in the scene.

Now we will introduce you how to take part in this study. Please tick the checkboxes to indicate that you have read the instructions.

First, you will see a picture, and a sentence describing the scene. Please imagine that you have a listener, who can also see this picture. Please read the sentence aloud, and then click on the sentence. The sentence will disappear.

Then you will see another picture, in which one of the toy characters had performed an action. You will need to produce a new sentence describing the action of the character and say the sentence aloud.

Please produce the new sentence in a way that, by listening to your sentence, the imaginary listener, who cannot see the second picture, can recreate the scene by moving the toys to which s/he has physical access.

After describing the action, you will need to click on the "Proceed" button.
